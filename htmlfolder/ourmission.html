Our Mission


Our mission is to restore and preserve the Ganges River for future generations. We aim to bring awareness and address the pollution and ecological challenges threatening this sacred river by raising awareness, promoting sustainable practices, and fostering community involvement.

The irrigation canals in the Ganga basin face challenges such as water wastage, pollution from agricultural runoff, and siltation. Additionally, with growing demands from agriculture, industry, and domestic use, there are concerns about the sustainability of water resources in the region.

Efforts to modernize irrigation systems and manage water resources more effectively, including using canal lining to reduce water loss, are essential for ensuring that the Ganga continues to support both agriculture and environmental health in the long term.

By uniting individuals, communities, and organizations, we strive to create impactful change through clean-up drives, educational campaigns, and partnerships with local authorities. Together, we can revive the Ganges and ensure its health and vitality for years to come.
